OUR AI

WHAT IS IT ?

Machine learning models for sentiment analysis, formality analysis, topic modelling, and expertise classification that make up our AI

RELATIONSHIPS:
Our sentiment Model processes positive/Negative sentiment from email conversations. Our formality Model processes Formal/Informal sentiment. Number of exchanges between sender and receiver as well as the time known contibute to relationship score.

INTEREST AND EXPERTISE:
Topic Modelling with LDA to extract interests and expertise and map them to people. Our intersest/expertise classifier distinguised between those interested in an entity and those who are experts in a domain.

DICTIONARY:
Named entity recognition using Spacy, Google KG API

Datasets

Enron Corporation was an American energy, commodities, and services company based in Houston, Texas. After the company collapsed, the emails of its employees were made public and this dataset of million emails were selected for our analysis.


An open source datsets of Tweets was used to train our random forest classifier to classify emails as formal or informal. We used tweets as the informal corpora.


TwentyNewsGroups is an open source dataset containing news from 20 different news categories. Most of these news articals are formal and was our formal corpora to perform our training.


The Amazon Product Review Dataset consists of nearly 70 million product reviews and this datset was used to train a generative m-LSTM Neural Network. One among the 4096 Neurons of this network specialized to perform sentiment analysis and this model was used to generate a sentiment score for both emails and entities.
